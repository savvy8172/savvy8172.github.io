The primary goal is communicated through the product images which you can interact with, the signup flashing text, and the information button in the top right corner of the webpage.

Encouraging ongoing engagement within the website, the minigame is implemented. Therefore whoever is on the site to get an update on the game's status, can be short term entertained.

Downstream is not majorly using mechanisms to draw site users back to their site as it is mostly used to display the games progress and sign you up to a waitlist to be updated.
